The wise men and the escape to Egypt

Scripture

Matt 2

Summary

When Jesus was born, the wise men saw His star in heaven and understood that a King was born. They followed the star and came to King Herod in order to find out in which city the Messiah was supposed to be born. The religious leaders read the words of prophecy, where it is written that He was born in Bethlehem. They found Jesus there, worshipped Him and gave him treasures (gold, frankincense and myrrh).

God warned the wise men to go home by another way. God also sent an angel to Joseph in a dream to tell him to get away to Egypt because Herod wanted to kill Jesus. They left in the night for Egypt.
