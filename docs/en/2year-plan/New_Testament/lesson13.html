Jesus was teaching the people about trusting God and loving others (Matt 5-7). That‚Äôs why He shared this parable about listening and doing His Word. He wants us to understand what it is like to really believe and live the truth. He explains what happens when we love God and follow His Word. I hear the Word of God at Sunday school. or maybe I read stories in a children‚Äôs Bible. When I hear and read, Jesus speaks to me. He not only wants me to hear interesting stories about Him but wants the things that I hear to change my heart. For example, when I hear that Jesus fed the people because He loved and cared for them, I remember that Jesus cares for me and I can ask Him to help me to care for others. If I have cookies, I can share them with someone and show them that I love them and want to care for them. I can tell them that Jesus loves them.

In this story, Jesus said that the rain and wind hit hard and destroyed the house that was built upon the sand. Rain and wind can represent problems in life. But people who don‚Äôt believe in Jesus are like the house built upon the sand. When everything is bad in life, they fall. Thank you that You help me to hear and fulfill Your Word, to Love You and others with my life.
