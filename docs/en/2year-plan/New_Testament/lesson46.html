Application

Jesus saves me and everyone who believes in Him by grace. Trying to fulfill the law doesn‚Äôt save me. I am wrong if I think that God will accept me if I do good works. God accepts those who put their faith in the perfect life, sacrifice and victory of Jesus. I trust in the righteousness of Jesus and not my ‚Äúrighteousness‚Äù. When I believe in Him, by grace, He forgives my sin and gives me a new heart that loves God and others. He gives me the Holy Spirit, who lives in me and helps me to live pleasing to God. The fruit of the Holy Spirit is love. When I love Jesus (and He helps me to love Him), then just like an apple tree that grows apples, so my life and my heart will be filled with love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness and self-control. I can ask Jesus to grow the fruit of the Holy Spirit in my life and that I would love Him and others more and more.

What does the fruit of the Holy Spirit look like? What do these look like: love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness and self-control? What examples of each one can I think of? Jesus is God and He showed us all of the fruit of the Spirit. Which examples from the life of Jesus can I remember that are examples of: love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness and self-control? Jesus, please grow the fruit of the Spirit in my life.
