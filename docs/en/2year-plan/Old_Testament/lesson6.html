God is Trustworthy

Old Testament #6

The Call of Abraham

Scripture

Gen. 12 and 15

Summary

God commanded Abram to go out from his land and his father‚Äôs house to a land that He would show him. He promised to make a great nation out of Abram and bless all the nations of the earth through him. Abram was obedient to God. He came to the land of Canaan. There God revealed Himself to Abram and promised to give that land to his descendants. Abram built an altar to the Lord. Later, when there was a famine (no food) in the land, Abram went to Egypt. He was afraid that the Egyptians would kill him and take his wife, Sarai. He asked her to say that she was his sister. People took Sarai to the house of Pharaoh anyway but God protected her and struck the household of Pharaoh because he had taken her. Pharaoh returned Sarai to Abram and asked him to leave.

God's character :

God is generous
God blesses his people
God protects His people
God is trustworthy

Application

God fulfilled His promise to Abraham and He fulfills His promise to me. God promised to bless everyone through Abraham. Jesus is a descendant of Abraham and through faith in Jesus, God blesses me. God gave Abraham land and He gives me a place in heaven with Him. I thank Jesus because He saves me.
