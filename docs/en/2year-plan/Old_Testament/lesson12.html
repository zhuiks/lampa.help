Summary

In Gen. 28:10-22 through Gen. 30:24, Jacob leaves his family because he is afraid that his brother will hurt him. He goes to his mom‚Äôs relatives who live far away. On the way there, God speaks to Jacob, showing him a ladder going from heaven to earth and promises to give Jacob the land that He promised to Abraham and to be with Jacob. Jacob goes to live with his uncle Laban, who tricks Jacob into marrying his older daughter instead of the younger daughter that he promised him. Jacob marries the younger daughter too. Gen 31-33: Jacob and his family leave Laban without saying goodbye. Laban was angry and ran after Jacob but God protected Jacob. Jacob and his family found a place to live and Jacob built an altar to worship God.

Application

God is with me. God fulfills His promises to me. What promises are in the Bible? In Jesus, He promises to love me, forgive my sin, bring me to heaven to live with him, give me the Holy Spirit for wisdom, help, strength and comfort. I trust Jesus. When I am afraid, I can ask Jesus for help and He helps me just like He helped Jacob when he was afraid of meeting his brother.
