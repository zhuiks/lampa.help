Application

Often in Deuteronomy, God tells the people to love Him. He also repeats that He loves them, that He chose them, that He leads them and that He is with them. I know that God loves me. He chose me and leads me and is always with me. God warns that it is dangerous to not trust Him.

Jesus, help me to trust You and follow after You.

Just like Moses, people die, but I am confident that I will be with God because I believe in Jesus. God gave Joshua a job to lead the people. He wants me to be brave and courageous and to trust Him just like Joshua.

The people of Israel were ready to enter the promised land. Maybe they were scared because they didn‚Äôt know what was ahead. God comforted them and told them that a blessing was waiting for them.
