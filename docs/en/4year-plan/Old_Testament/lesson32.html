Old Testament #32

The pillar of cloud and of fire

Scripture

Num. 1-10 esp. chapt 9

Summary

Chapt. 1-4: God commanded them to count all the men (20 years and older) from each tribe and to count the Levites because they would serve in the tabernacle and carry it when they journeyed to a new place. God told the people how to set up camp around the tabernacle. God explained how the Levites would serve. Only the sons of Aaron could offer sacrifices for the people. The other Levites helped to serve in the tabernacle. God required the redemption of every firstborn in Israel.
