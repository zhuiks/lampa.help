Application

God made Adam and Eve in His image. He made me in His image! What does this mean? It means that because God is the Creator, I like to create, because God is Spirit, I am spiritual, because God is the Communicator, I communicate, because God has a Reason and Feelings, I think and feel, because God is Relational, I have relationships.* People‚Äôs good characteristics can reflect God. Reason, creativity, emotions, skills, beauty, love and honesty are all from God and show us what our God is like.

Adam and Eve were the first people and the first husband and wife. God gives unity to a husband and wife so that they can be together as a family.

God is so good and gave me life (physical). It‚Äôs right that I would love Him most of all, worship Him and follow all that He says, obeying Him. He created us for fellowship with Him, which is spiritual life. Our sin separates us from fellowship with God. But through faith in Jesus, I can have fellowship with God again. This fellowship is called eternal life. (Jn. 17:3 ‚ÄúAnd this is eternal life, that they may know You, the only true God, and Jesus Christ whom You have sent.‚Äù). Dear God, thank you that you gave me life! Help me to listen to you and love you. Please help me to show your love and goodness to others, that they would know how wonderful you are! Amen.

Note: This is a good opportunity to ask the children if they have eternal (spiritual) life through faith in Jesus and share the gospel with them. May God save the children that you teach and give them life in Jesus!
