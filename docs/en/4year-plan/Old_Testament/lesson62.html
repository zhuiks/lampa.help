Old Testament #62

Building the temple

Scripture

1 Kings 5-9, 2 Chron. 2-8

Summary

Ch. 5: God provided everything for building the temple.

Ch. 6: Solomon built the temple (the place of worship) for God in Jerusalem. It was big and beautiful. Many people over many years built the temple. God spoke to Solomon and warned him to follow Him (v. 11-13).

Ch. 7: Solomon built his house. Hiram made everything that was needed for ministry in the temple.

Ch. 8: When the temple was finished being built, all the people gathered to worship God. They brought the ark of the covenant into the temple. Inside the ark were the tablets with the 10 commandments. The priests offered sacrifices to God and the Lord‚Äôs glory filled the temple. Solomon prayed to God and thanked Him that He fulfilled His Word to the people. He commanded the people to always follow God and asked God to answer their prayers in the temple. The people celebrated for 7 days.

Ch. 9: God again spoke to Solomon and said that He heard his prayer. He warned Solomon to follow Him and said that the temple would be destroyed if the people walked away from God (v. 1-9).
