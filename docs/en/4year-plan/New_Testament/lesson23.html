Application

I believe that Jesus is the Messiah. He is the Savior who forgives my sin and gives me the Holy Spirit. I believe that Jesus has authority and I believe His Word, the Bible.

People had different opinions about Jesus. Some people believed in Him and others wanted to kill Him. Do I believe in Him? Maybe some people are angry with me because of my faith in Jesus. Jesus, help me to not be afraid of their opinions but to find my strength in You to love them and to boldly live for You.

Do I thirst? Just as water is needed for physical life, so Jesus is needed for spiritual life. I believe that I can come to Jesus and He will give me His Holy Spirit. His Holy Spirit changes me and will be in me forever, like a spring or fountain.

Jesus offers salvation to all and wants all people to come to Him, the Messiah and Savior. His Holy Spirit is within me and I also want to invite people to come to Jesus for life.
