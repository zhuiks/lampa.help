Application

I believe that Jesus is the Son of God. He is greater than the prophets, the angels, Moses, Joshua and the High Priests. Everything in the Old Testament (the Law, the tabernacle, the sacrifices and the priesthood) point to Jesus. I deserve death and punishment for my sin against the Holy God. People earlier brought sacrifices of animals according to the law so that the blood of the animal would cover their sin. But the priests needed to constantly bring sacrifices for themselves and the people because the blood of animals could not give them a new heart and new relationship with God. Jesus is God and man. He never sinned and gave Himself as a sacrifice for my sin. This is why He is the perfect sacrifice and could pay for my sin once and for all. He always lives to intercede for me.

How does this truth change my life? I trust the sacrifice of Jesus for my salvation and not my works according to the law. He gives me rest through faith in His sacrifice. He helps me to love others and trust the Word. When I‚Äôm scared and people are against me, I can stand firm in my faith in Jesus. I know that He is the One Savior. I know that He is my Salvation. I know that He will come soon and I will be with Him forever. Jesus, You are a Great Savior. Thank you for Your perfect sacrifice. Help me to stand firm in faith even when I suffer because of it. Thank you that through Jesus, I can come boldly to God for grace and help. You live forever and are always my help. Praise to You!
