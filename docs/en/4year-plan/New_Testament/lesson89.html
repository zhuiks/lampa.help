Application

In Jesus, I have a living hope. I know that He will come to take me to Himself to live eternally with Him in heaven. When life here is difficult, I can remember that I have a heavenly inheritance (eternal life with Jesus) awaiting me. I expect His return. I am thankful that He paid for my sin with His own blood and this is why I want to live in purity as I wait for Jesus.

It‚Äôs important for me to know what God says to me in the Word because the Word is eternal. In the Word, I can find out the truth and promises of God. People without Jesus live however they want and sin against God. Their behavior is very dangerous and they harm themselves and those around them. Jesus helps me to live holy because He gave me the Holy Spirit. I rely upon His strength to live pleasing to God. He helps me to love others. When people see that I love others, they can understand how Jesus loves them. Jesus, help me to be loving.
