Summary

Jesus spoke to the people and explained to them who He is. He talked about shepherds, their work, responsibilities and how they treat their sheep. A good shepherd will take good care of his sheep and protect them from enemies. Sheep gather into the sheep pen to rest. Jesus said that He is the door to the place of rest. Jesus says that He is the Good Shepherd. A shepherd speaks to his sheep that they know his voice and follow him. There are robbers that only want to hurt the sheep (v. 10).
