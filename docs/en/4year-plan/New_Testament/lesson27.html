Application

Jesus traveled around to teach people and help them. He wanted people to be a part of His kingdom. Jesus sent His disciples out to do what He did. Today, Jesus calls people to be a part of His kingdom. He wants to teach and help people to understand that He is Savior and King. Through me, He wants to tell people about His love and power.

Just as Jesus freely saved me by His grace, so I can freely help people and tell them about Jesus. He gives me His Holy Spirit so I can know what to say. He gives me boldness to not fear people, but to fear (that is, to respect and love) God. People can kill the body, but they can‚Äôt take my salvation. My physical life on earth will end one day, but eternal life in the knowledge of Jesus is forever! Some people hate Jesus, some people will hate me when I follow Jesus. Jesus Himself endured hatred from people. He understands and helps me. Jesus gave His Life to save us. He didn‚Äôt live in a palace and didn‚Äôt take money when He preached. I should be like Jesus. He is my example in all things. Jesus promises that in heaven, He will reward those who love Him and others. I show my love towards Jesus and others when I do what Jesus did (invite people to come to Him for salvation, tell them about His Kingdom, so that Jesus would become their king, and help those in need in the name of Jesus.)
