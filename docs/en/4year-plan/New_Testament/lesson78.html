Application

My faith in Jesus changes me. Before I believed in Jesus, I was a slave to sin and I had no power to do what is right. But now, through faith in Jesus, I can live with Him. That is, His Holy Spirit lives in me and gives me new desires (to love God and others), and new life. The Life of Jesus in in me.

Sin controlled me before. Even if I didn‚Äôt really want to sin, I didn‚Äôt have strength to resist sin. But now, Jesus is My Lord and His Holy Spirit lives in me. I want to live for Jesus. I can ask Him for help and He will give me strength to act according to the Spirit. The Holy Spirit frees me from sin and death through faith in Jesus. In Jesus, I have a new nature, a new direction and a new destination!

All people without Jesus are dead in their sins. We all inherited the sinful nature of Adam. But believers in Jesus are given new life. He makes believers alive. Through faith in Jesus, by His grace and mercy, He gives us forgiveness, justification (that is, we are made right according to God‚Äôs Law), and the hope of glory. I know that despite the difficulties in this life, Jesus promised to receive me into glory. Nothing can separate me from His love. Jesus is always with me and His promises are firm and faithful. Jesus, help me to reckon myself dead to sin and alive for God in Jesus Christ, my Lord.
