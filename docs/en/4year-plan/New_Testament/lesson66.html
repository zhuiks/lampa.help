Application

The fruit of the Holy Spirit is love. When I love Jesus (and He helps me to love Him), then just like an apple tree that grows apples, so my life (and my heart) will be filled with love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness and self-control. I can ask Jesus to grow the fruit of the Holy Spirit in my life and that I would love Him and others more and more.

What does the fruit of the Holy Spirit look like? What do these look like: love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness and self-control? What examples of each one can I think of? Jesus is God and He showed us all of the fruit of the Spirit. Which examples from the life of Jesus can I remember that are examples of: love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness and self-control? Do I believe that Jesus can help this fruit to grow in my life? He wants to help me. I can ask of HIm. Jesus, please fill me with Your Holy Spirit and grow the fruit of the Spirit in my life. Help me to live pleasing to You and to love You.

Paul warned believers to not try to come to God through the works of the law. I can only come to God through faith in Jesus Christ, trusting in His righteousness. This is why I boast in the cross. ‚ÄúBut God forbid that I should boast except in the cross of our Lord Jesus Christ, by whom the world has been crucified to me, and I to the world.‚Äù (6:14) On the cross, Jesus died for my sin and paid for my iniquities. I want to live pleasing to Jesus, so that people would see that He is right. He is the Loving Victor who makes us God‚Äôs children by His grace and not according to my works. I want to be a part of God‚Äôs family and tell people that they also can become God‚Äôs children through faith in Jesus.
