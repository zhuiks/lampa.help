Application

Nicodemus came to Jesus with questions. I also can ask God when I don‚Äôt understand something. Most often, Jesus answers me through the Bible. The Bible is the truth and within it are the answers to the questions about God, the world, and people.

Jesus shared an example from the history of Israel when the people had sinned and God told them to look at a bronze snake hanging on a pole (Num. 21:4-9). Those who looked up at the bronze snake on the pole, where their sin was judged, were saved by God. When I look to Jesus in faith, believing that He died on the cross for my sin and took my punishment upon Himself, He saves me and gives me new life. When I believe in Jesus I am not judged by God for my sin. He conquered sin and death. He forgives my sin.

I know that God loves me because He sent Jesus to die for me. I want to live in the light, that is, live honestly and openly before God and not hide from Him. I want to tell people about the love of Jesus, so that they would also look to Jesus and receive salvation through faith in Him. Jesus is the only way of salvation.
