Application

I know that Jesus is God. He was with God the Father and the Holy Spirit when they created everything. He gave me the Holy Spirit and Jesus lives within me. His presence with me is the hope of glory. That is, I am certain that i will be with Him eternally in heaven (in glory) because right now He is with me. This is amazing news. I am very thankful to Jesus that He with with me now.
