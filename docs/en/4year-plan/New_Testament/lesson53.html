Application

Jesus gave believers His Holy Spirit just as He promised. The Holy Spirit gave the believers power to witness about Jesus. This passage talks about the baptism of the Holy Spirit. I see that Jesus wants to pour out the Holy Spirit on each believer. The Holy Spirit always gives glory to Jesus and not to people. The Holy Spirit helps people to understand that Jesus is Saviour, God and King. John 16:8-11 also explains the work of the Holy Spirit.

__Jesus, fill me with the Holy Spirit and help me to share with people about You. Thank you that You are my help. Just as you gave Peter words to share with people, please give me words when I need them. Help me to rely upon You.__

Jesus created and builds the Church. The Church is a group of people who love Jesus and follow Him. I am a part of the Church when I believe in Jesus and fellowship with other believers. People that are part of the Church not only read the Bible, pray but they also love Jesus and one another by sharing their lives with one another. Believers often gather together with one another. Others see our lives and how we love Jesus and one another. Often they want to know Jesus too after they see this love. God, bless our church and save even more people through us.
